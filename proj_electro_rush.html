Electro Rush project was to promote a music event with an international scope that would take place in the Montreal area. This event would be an opportunity for artists to gain visibility and share their original musical creations with audiences. This unifying event would encompass certain peripheral activities that would make it possible to simultaneously showcase regional talent and local businesses.

Guidelines and constraints of the project

  • All elements must align with the style corresponding to the concept of the event.
  • Draw the inspiration from the visual templates presented or other sources of inspiration.
  • Develop an inclusive graphic concept that will serve as a common thread throughout all the documents.
