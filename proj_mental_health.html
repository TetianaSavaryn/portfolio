The Canadian Mental Health Association - Quebec Division has expressed a need to create visuals for a web and print campaign to promote "Mental Health Week 2020". I had to provide an outstanding graphic design service that would meet the unique needs of client’s corporate personality and Quebec citizens.

The style of the visuals for a web and print campaign to promote "Mental Health Week 2020" was a package that reflected both the mental health study area and specific youth culture of Quebec. I used Quebec historical identity and specifically the culture of it’s citizens of 25-35 years old. I wanted young locals to have a strong sense of relation when they get information about mental health and depression in young age.

The visuals to be designed in the following formats: poster, leaflet, web banner, bookmark and the black and white version of the poster as an advertisement in The Gazette.

Poster design

Size: 11" x 17"

To achieve the clear and understandable explanation of the main idea – the prevention of depression in young adult age – it was decided to use illustrations, simple shapes and pastel colors combined with a bright yellow color for the text blocks. I outlined the alignment proportions of the text and background: 10-15% - the text area, 20% - main slogan.

The illustration should have been simple and clear, without too many elements and overdetailed textures / shapes.

The slogan must have been short, relatable and motivational. I avoided any negative statements and ambiguous meanings. Finally, it had to propose an alternative choice.

Leaflet design

Size: 8.5” x 11”

The composition consists of three levels (rows). The top level is the part of a background, the idea of life outside the depressive state. It shows young people lliving their lives on top of the “hole”. I used pastel colors and avoided any outlines to make those illustrations less sharp.

The central area is the illustration of a young woman – a main character of the poster who represents a depressive personality. She is illustrated more detailed, I used some shadows and outlines. The main slogan and message of the poster are situated on the same level, being inseparable from the main character.

The bottom part – footer with all necessary information provided by my client.
