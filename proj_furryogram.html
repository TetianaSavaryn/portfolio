A heartbeat monitor for your furry friend, that turns your phone into easy-to-use device, to record, compare and save the data of your pet’s heart rate.

Track your pet’s health and well-being! It’s like a real cardiogram - see (and hear!) every heart beat.

Furryogram’s revolutionary software allows any cat or dog owner to monitor pet’s rate using phone’s microphone. Recordings can be checked and marked on scale 1-5 depending on pet’s activity to identify high and low pulse rates.

PLEASE NOTE: This is not a substitute for care from a professional veterinarian.
